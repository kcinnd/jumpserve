Mastermind Game

How to Play

Welcome to Mastermind! Your goal is to guess the secret code of five volleyballs Here's how:

  • The secret code consists of 5 volleyballs, each possibly one of 10 colors.
  • Each guess must also consist of 5 volleyballs
  • After each guess, you'll receive feedback in the form of black and white pegs:
    • A black peg indicates a volleyball of the right color in the correct position.
    • A white peg indicates a volleyball of the right color but in the wrong position.
  • You have 8 chances to guess the code correctly.
  • To make a guess, select colors from the options at the bottom and place them in the blank spots on the current row. Then, press "Submit Guess".
  • If all volleyballs in a guess are correct in both color and position, you win!
  • If you use all 8 guesses without solving the code, the game resets.

Good luck, and have fun!
